Abstract

The present course - intended for a mixed audience of mathematicians and physicists - provides a gentle introduction to the the basic ideas TBA

Outline

Lecture 1:

  • crashcourse symplectic geometry and MW-reduction in 15 minutes.
  • introduction to multisymplectic manifolds
  • Leibniz algebra structure and Caseys reduction
  • Lie-infinity algebra

Lecture 2:

  • de Rham algebra of a commutative algebra (as above)
  • constraint algebras, modules etc.
  • constraint triples and the corresponding reduction of vector fields and forms
  • algebraic multisymplectic reduction, residue defect

Lecture 3:

  • Algebraic Reduction from covariant momentum maps
  • Example of multisymplectic reduction
  • constraint triples and the corresponding reduction of vector fields and forms
  • symplectic example

Lecture 4:

  • systematic treatment of the symplectic case
